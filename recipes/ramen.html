Ramen

What is a delicious Ramen ?

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup with flavorful broth, chewy noodles, and assorted toppings like sliced pork, soft-boiled eggs, and green onions. It's enjoyed for its comforting warmth and delicious taste, available in various regional styles across Japan.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of instant ramen noodles
  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of miso paste
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish
  • Soft-boiled eggs, sliced, for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add the instant ramen noodles to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions, usually about 3-4 minutes.
  3. While the noodles are cooking, stir in the soy sauce, miso paste, and sesame oil.
  4. Once the noodles are cooked, remove the pot from heat.
  5. Serve the ramen in bowls, garnished with sliced green onions, soft-boiled eggs, and any other toppings you like.
  6. Enjoy your delicious homemade ramen!
